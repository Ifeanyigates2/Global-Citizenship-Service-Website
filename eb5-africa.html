EB-5 Africa

The EB-5 visa offers the fastest and most simple route to a Green Card for individuals and families from countries across Africa. There is no sponsorship requirement and EB5 visa holders have the freedom to live, work, retire, invest or study anywhere in the United States.

The EB-5 visa has grown significantly in popularity in Africa with South Africa registering a sixfold increase in the last five years and Nigeria tripling the number of EB-5 visas issued to its citizens over the same period.

Regional Centers

There are three main requirements for EB-5 visa applicants. They need to be able to invest a minimum of $500,000, they must make that investment in a new commercial venture, and their investment must create ten full-time American jobs. Applicants can decide whether to invest in their own project or invest in a government-approved Regional Center project.

Regional Centers usually invest in Real Estate projects across America. The investment is naturally at risk, so it is vital to conduct due diligence on your investment.
