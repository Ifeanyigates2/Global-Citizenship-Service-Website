About Us

Global Citizenship Services is the leading services provider for all your global relocation needs. Through our exclusive partnerships with some of the best immigration and corporate attorneys in the world, we are able to offer complete solutions for a range of global Citizenship by Investment programs, as well as EB-5 and E-2 investor visas to the United States. Our bespoke solutions are designed to make your move as effortless as possible, and include visa support, tax advice, corporate work, and help finding a business or property in your chosen destination.
