EB-5 India

The EB-5 visa offers the fastest, most-effective route to a Green Card for Indian citizens. The EB-5 visa has boomed in popularity in India in recent years as people take advantage of the opportunity of living, working and retiring anywhere in America.

Many of our Indian clients are motivated to apply for EB-5 because it offers access to in-state tuition for their children, as well as the ability to work after graduation without the need for an H-1B visa. The EB-5 visa has become so popular, that applications from India are now subject to a waiting list.

Nevertheless, EB-5 remains one of the best options for Indian nations seeking to move to the United States. For clients who wish to move to the United States while they are on the waiting list, we are able to discuss a range of options.

Regional Centers

The EB-5 visa requires an investment of $500,000 in a business or project that creates and sustains ten American jobs. There are two options available, either to create a business yourself or to invest in a Regional Center, which uses your money to invest in a construction project such as a hotel or apartment block. Whichever you choose, we are on hand to support you.

The Regional Center options is by far the most popular because they take care of compliance to reduce the risk to your visa and your investment. There are over 800 Regional Centers across the United States of varying quality. We are able to help you navigate this complex web and determine the best option for you.
