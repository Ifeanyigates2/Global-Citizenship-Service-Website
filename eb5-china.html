EB-5 China

The EB-5 Visa is the fastest and most effective route to a US Green Card. It has proven so popular in China that thousands of applications have been filed. Such high demand coupled with an annual quota on each country, means that Chinese citizens currently face a wait of more than a decade for an application filed today.

At GCS we are in the business of finding solutions to our clients' global mobility needs. We are able to help our clients explore options to allow you to live and work in the United States on a non-immigrant basis while you are on the EB-5 waitlist.

Retrogression Solution

The E-2 Visa offers a way to live in the United States for the purpose of running a business while on the EB-5 waitlist. Chinese citizens are not currently eligible for E-2 Visas, so we can help you first become a citizen of a country that is eligible.

The E-2 visa requires the applicant to present a business plan for owning and operating a business in the United States. We can help you identify a business based upon your interests and budget and we can connect you to the best US immigration lawyers to file your case.
