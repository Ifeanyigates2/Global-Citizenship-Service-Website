Regional Centers

For those who can afford the minimum $500,000 investment, the EB-5 visa is the fastest route to a Green Card. This visa allows the applicant, a spouse, and dependants under the age of 21 to live, work and study in the United States.

It is the fastest route to a green card currently available to Russian nationals. There are two routes to the EB-5 Visa. A direct EB-5 where you can own and operate your own business so long as it meets certain criteria. Or you can invest with a Regional Center which take responsibility for compliance, freeing you to work, retire, study or run a business.
